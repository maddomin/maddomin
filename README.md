# Manuel Domínguez Pino

🦠 PhD researcher in Microbiology and Parasitology  
🧬 Molecular epidemiology of lymphogranuloma venereum (LGV) in Madrid  
💻 Microbial genomics · molecular epidemiology · bioinformatics

I work on microbial genomics and molecular epidemiology, with a particular focus on *Chlamydia trachomatis*, whole-genome sequencing from direct clinical samples, recombinant variant analysis, phylogenomics, and 16S metagenomics.

  - Molecular epidemiology of lymphogranuloma venereum (LGV)

  - De novo assembly, quality control, MLST and *ompA* typing
  - Phylogenomics and molecular evolution
